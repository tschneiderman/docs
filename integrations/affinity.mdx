[Affinity](https://www.affinity.co) is a relationship intelligence CRM built for dealmakers. It automatically captures relationship history from your team's emails and calendar and surfaces who knows who across your network.

## What syncs

When you connect Affinity, Breaknine imports:

- **People** — contacts from your lists, including emails, titles, and companies
- **Organizations** — company records with firmographic data
- **List entries** — deal pipeline stages and field values
- **Relationship history** — interaction signals captured by Affinity

All synced records appear as prospects in Breaknine and are available for AI enrichment and outreach.

## Connect Affinity

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your API key">
    In Affinity, go to **Settings → API** and generate a new API key. Copy it to your clipboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect in Breaknine">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations** in Breaknine and click **Connect** next to Affinity. Paste your API key and click **Save**.

    Alternatively, you can import contacts directly via a CSV export from Affinity — choose **Upload CSV** in the same dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your sync">
    After connecting, select which Affinity lists to sync into Breaknine. You can map list fields to Breaknine prospect attributes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sync behavior

| Type | Behavior |
|---|---|
| Initial sync | All records from selected lists are imported on first connection |
| Incremental sync | New and updated records sync on a scheduled basis |
| Credentials | API key, encrypted at rest |

## Disconnect

Go to **Settings → Integrations**, click the Affinity row, and select **Disconnect**. You can optionally delete all synced data from Breaknine at the same time.
