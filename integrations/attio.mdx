[Attio](https://attio.com) is a modern, flexible CRM built for relationship-driven teams. It lets you build custom objects, views, and workflows on top of your contact and company data.

## What syncs

When you connect Attio, Breaknine imports:

- **People** — contact records with emails, job titles, and linked companies
- **Companies** — organization records with attributes and relationship signals
- **Custom attributes** — any custom fields you've defined in Attio are mapped to Breaknine prospect fields

All synced records appear as prospects in Breaknine and are available for AI enrichment and outreach.

## Connect Attio

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your API key">
    In Attio, go to **Settings → API keys** and create a new key with read access. Copy it to your clipboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect in Breaknine">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations** in Breaknine and click **Connect** next to Attio. Enter your API key and click **Save**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your sync">
    After connecting, choose which objects and lists to include in your sync. Map Attio attributes to Breaknine fields as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sync behavior

| Type | Behavior |
|---|---|
| Initial sync | All people and companies are imported on first connection |
| Incremental sync | New and updated records sync on a scheduled basis |
| Credentials | API key, encrypted at rest |

## Disconnect

Go to **Settings → Integrations**, click the Attio row, and select **Disconnect**. You can optionally delete all synced data from Breaknine at the same time.
