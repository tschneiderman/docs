[Airtable](https://airtable.com) is a flexible spreadsheet-database hybrid used by many teams to track contacts, deals, and pipelines. Breaknine connects via OAuth so you don't need to manage API keys manually.

## What syncs

When you connect Airtable, Breaknine can import records from any base and table you select:

- **Contact tables** — people records with name, email, title, and company fields
- **Company tables** — organization records with firmographic fields
- **Custom fields** — any field in your Airtable table can be mapped to a Breaknine prospect attribute

All synced records appear as prospects in Breaknine and are available for AI enrichment and outreach.

## Connect Airtable

<Steps>
  <Step title="Authorize via OAuth">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations** in Breaknine and click **Connect** next to Airtable. You'll be redirected to Airtable to authorize access. Grant Breaknine permission to read your bases and click **Allow access**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your bases and tables">
    After authorizing, choose which Airtable base and table contains your contacts or companies.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map your fields">
    Map the columns in your Airtable table to Breaknine's prospect fields (name, email, company, title, etc.).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sync behavior

| Type | Behavior |
|---|---|
| Initial sync | All rows from selected tables are imported on first connection |
| Incremental sync | New and updated rows sync on a scheduled basis |
| Auth method | OAuth — no API key needed |

<Note>
  Airtable uses OAuth for authentication. Breaknine never stores your Airtable password. You can revoke access at any time from your Airtable account under **Account → Authorized apps**.
</Note>

## Disconnect

Go to **Settings → Integrations**, click the Airtable row, and select **Disconnect**. You can optionally delete all synced data from Breaknine at the same time.
