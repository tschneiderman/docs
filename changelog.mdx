<Update label="Apr 16, 2026" description="v0.5.0">
  ### Outreach from the prospect page, prospect intelligence, and command palette updates

  **Outreach generation from a prospect's page**

  You can now draft and send outreach messages without leaving a prospect's detail page. A new **Outreach** tab is available on each prospect page — generate a message, copy it, or send it directly via email. Outreach is generated using the prospect's enriched data including company context, contacts, and recent news. You can also see a version history of all of the changes you've asked the outreach chat to make to your email.

  <video controls width="100%">
    <source src="https://pub-9bba086e01f6491e9984367c6bed7fc0.r2.dev/Outreach.mp4" type="video/mp4" />
  </video>

  **Founders on the prospect overview page**

  The prospect overview page now surfaces founder and key contact information — pulled from CRM contact info, your emails (if you've enabled this), and web search. You no longer have to navigate to a separate tab to find out who to reach out to. Contacts are displayed alongside company details as soon as you open the page.

  ![Founders on prospect overview page](/media/source%20badge.png)

  **Source badges on activity feed updates**

  Each update card in the activity feed now shows a small inline badge identifying where the update came from — Substack, company blog, or web/news. You can see the source platform at a glance without needing to click through to the original link.

  ![Source badges on activity feed updates](/media/Founders%20on%20prospect%20view.png)
  
  **Command palette redesign (⌘+K)**

  The ⌘+K command palette has been reorganized into two distinct sections: **Navigation** (go to any page in the app) and **Actions** (create prospects, trigger outreach, generate messages, and more). Keyboard shortcuts are now shown inline next to relevant actions, and previously missing destinations and commands have been added so ⌘+K works as a complete control hub across the app.

<Update label="Apr 7, 2026" description="v0.4.0">
  ### How to integrate, connect, and utilize new features

  **Gmail Integration**

  You can connect your work Gmail via **Settings > Integrations > Connect to Gmail**. You will see the security screen because we are currently in the audit process with Google, but you can bypass by pressing "Go to Breaknine" in the bottom left corner.

  </video>

  **Proactive Outreach Suggestions**

  Breaknine is now surfacing proactive outreach suggestions based on your proprietary notes on a prospect, their publicly available information, and triggers you've set for re-engagement. You can see proactive outreaches on the home screen and also on the "Suggestions" tab at the top of the "Tasks" page.

  **Portfolio Company Tracking**

  We are now tracking portfolio company updates in the same way we are tracking prospects. Add your portfolio companies by going to **Settings > Company > Portfolio**. Then you'll see all of your portcos in the "Portfolio" tab on the left panel. You'll also be able to filter by only portco updates at the top of the homescreen.

<Update label="Mar 30, 2026" description="v0.3.0">
  ### Google Workspace integration, proactive updates, and daily email digest organization

  **Google Workspace integration — email and calendar sync**

  You can now connect your Google Workspace account to sync both Gmail and Google Calendar. Once connected, Breaknine pulls in relevant email threads and calendar events tied to your deals, so your activity feed and notes stay in sync with your actual work without any manual entry. We are actively working on an Outlook integration as well. 

  **Proactive updates from note context and emails**

  Breaknine now surfaces proactive deal updates & reach out triggers based on the content of your notes and email threads. When relevant signals are detected — a note that tells you to reach out when a certain milestone is hit and a monthly newsletter stating some semblance of that milestone - you'll see a suggested update in your feed so nothing slips through the cracks.

  **Portfolio company tracking**

  Portfolio company updates are tracked the same way prospects are - ensuring you have a pulse on all of the happenings of your portcos. You can add portfolio companies directly in the Breaknine app.

  **Competitor page updates**

  You can now add competitors of your prospects - so you have a 360 degree view of what is happening with your prospects. Breaknine pulls competitors from call notes & suggests potential competitors. Competitor pages have been updated with improved layout and more consistent data display. Key metrics and recent activity are easier to scan, and navigation between related companies is more fluid.

  **Daily digest organization**

  The daily digest has been further refined. Sections are more clearly delineated, and the digest now incorporates signals from your synced email and calendar alongside the existing trigger updates.
</Update>
