<Update label="Mar 20, 2026" description="v0.2.0">
  ### Daily digest redesign, home feed improvements, and competitor reliability

  **Daily digest now organized into sections**

  Your daily update email has been restructured for clarity. Trigger updates are now grouped into dedicated sections — prospects, competitors, and portfolio — so you can scan what matters at a glance. A new **Next Actions** section surfaces tasks by urgency: past due, due today, and upcoming.

  **Home feed tab reorder and empty states**

  The activity feed tabs are now ordered **All → Prospects → Competitors → Portfolio**, matching how most users scan their pipeline. Each tab also shows a contextual empty state message when there's nothing to display, instead of a blank screen.

  **Competitor discovery reliability improvements**

  Hardened the competitor workflow with better cross-provider fallback handling and fixed a retry loop bug in candidate discovery. Competitor results are more consistent and load faster.
</Update>
