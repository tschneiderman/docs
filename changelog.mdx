<Update label="Mar 11, 2026">
  ### Interface polish

  Refined UI details, motion, and visual spacing across the app for a more polished experience. Interactions feel tighter and transitions are smoother throughout.

  ### Prospects label renamed to "Discovered"

  The **Review** label on prospect records has been renamed to **Discovered** to better reflect where contacts sit in your pipeline.

  ### Improvements

  - Improved cross-provider AI fallback handling so enrichment is more resilient when one model is unavailable
  - Fixed a retry loop in candidate discovery that could cause repeated redundant requests
  - Removed debug logging from semantic search for cleaner performance in production
</Update>

<Update label="Mar 5, 2026" description="v0.1.0" tags={["Launch"]}>
  ### Breaknine is live

  AI-powered prospect enrichment, CRM sync, and outreach — all in one place.

  - Connect Affinity, Attio, and Airtable
  - Enrich prospects with AI-generated summaries
  - Draft and send outreach from within the app
</Update>
