<Update label="Mar 30, 2026" description="v0.3.0">
  ### Google Workspace integration, suggested portfolio companies, and proactive updates

  **Google Workspace integration — email and calendar sync**

  You can now connect your Google Workspace account to sync both Gmail and Google Calendar. Once connected, Breaknine pulls in relevant email threads and calendar events tied to your deals, so your activity feed and notes stay in sync with your actual work without any manual entry.

  **Proactive updates from note context and email discussions**

  Breaknine now surfaces proactive deal updates based on the content of your notes and email threads. When relevant signals are detected — a portfolio company mentioned in a thread, a follow-up implied by a note — you'll see a suggested update in your feed so nothing slips through.

  **Suggested portfolio companies**

  The portfolio section now surfaces suggested companies you may want to track, based on your existing portfolio relationships and deal history. Review and add them in one click to keep your portfolio view complete.

  **Home feed tab improvements**

  The feed tabs have been refined with updated filtering logic and smoother loading states. Switching between All, Prospects, Competitors, and Portfolio is faster, and active filters persist as you navigate.

  **Portfolio and competitor page updates**

  Portfolio and competitor detail pages have been updated with improved layout and more consistent data display. Key metrics and recent activity are easier to scan, and navigation between related companies is more fluid.

  **Daily digest organization**

  The daily digest has been further refined. Sections are more clearly delineated, and the digest now incorporates signals from your synced email and calendar alongside the existing trigger updates.
</Update>
